maintaining a repertoire is no easy task. i find it helpful to divide it into four main parts:

  • - L3 (ready for recital)
  • - L2 (needs polishing)
  • - L1 (needs reading and polishing)
  • - L0 (start from scratch)

  • > Debussy, Arabesque No.1
